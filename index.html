Discovery Process 1:

The discovery process is done over WVS data set from the year 1995-2014, with almost 30 counties and on nearly 8 variable question in which the main concerned research is over the level of education correlation with other variables such us Children upbringing.

Assumption/Standardization/Notes:

  • All the data are excluded from “Don not know(DN)/Non Applicable(AN)”
  • “Not mentioned” response is considered the option to its positive counterpart. In short, people avoiding it to answer

Limitation of WVS analysis tool

  • The charts are limited to bar charts
  • At max 5 years of data can be view at a time
  • Can not compare more that two variables at a time
  • For more countries selected even variables data point can not be comapred at a time

First, the most thing which is noticed is a relation with education level on two extremes of having no education at all and have a University education. The first variable which is analyzed is “Children qualities: Religious faith”. And it is observed that with the increase of both education and years people seem less interested in teaching their children about religion. But there is an exception in these data which can be visible clearly that most of the Muslim countries are still give importance to religion than any other countries.

Now look over what is the status of religion to these countries, again we can see the Muslim states vote religion more important irrespective of education. But, as the year passed we see that more of the Christian states are giving more importance to the religion, which we also see in a previous analysis of Childern qualities, hence the influence of religion is increasing with time.
